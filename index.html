Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 1, 2030

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: Your name, username, email, FIL phone number, and optional bio and profile photo.
  • Messages: Text and images you send are stored in Firebase Realtime Database.
  • Online presence: We track when you are online to show your status to people you chat with.
  • Match data: Who you've seen, skipped, or connected with on the Match screen.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your account and enable messaging.
  • To show your profile in the Match discovery queue.
  • To generate and display your unique FIL number and shareable link.
  • To provide online/offline status to your chat partners.
  • We do not sell your data to advertisers.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Realtime Database) and images are stored on Cloudinary. By using Fall I Love, you consent to your data being processed by these services.

4. Visibility

  • Your name, FIL number, bio, and photo may be visible to other Fall I Love users — in chats, groups, the Match screen, and via your shareable link.
  • Your email address is never displayed to other users.
  • Messages in a conversation are visible only to the members of that conversation.

5. Your Rights

  • You may update your profile name, bio, and photo at any time.
  • You may stop using Fall I Love and delete your account at any time.
  • You may contact us to request deletion of your data.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser session storage only to remember whether you dismissed install prompts. No advertising cookies are used.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of Fall I Love after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us through the app. Fall I Love is a demonstration platform built on Firebase.
