Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 1, 2030

1. Information We Collect

  • Account info: Name, username, email, FIL number, bio, profile photo.
  • Messages: Text and images you send — stored in Firebase.
  • Presence: Online/offline status shown to chat partners.
  • Match data: Who you've seen, skipped, or connected with.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your account and enable messaging.
  • To show your profile in the Match discovery queue.
  • To generate your FIL number and shareable link.
  • We do not sell your data to advertisers.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored in Google Firebase Realtime Database. Images are stored on Cloudinary. By using Fall I Love, you consent to data being processed by these services.

4. Visibility

  • Name, FIL number, bio, and photo may be visible to other users.
  • Your email address is never shown to other users.
  • Messages are only visible to conversation members.

5. Your Rights

You may update your profile or delete your account at any time.

6. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us through the app.
