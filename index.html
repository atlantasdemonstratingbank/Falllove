About Fall I Love

Version 1.0 · 2025

What is Fall I Love?

Fall I Love is a next-generation private messenger built for real human connection. We believe communication should feel personal, beautiful, and safe — not like a corporate product.

Unlike other messengers, Fall I Love gives every user a unique FIL Number — a permanent identity you carry across devices, friendships, and time. Think of it like a phone number that belongs to you, not your carrier.

Our Mission

We're building the messenger we always wanted — one that respects your attention, protects your privacy, and makes every conversation feel like it matters.

Match Feature

Fall I Love's Match screen lets you discover new people organically. Swipe to connect, and when both sides match, a conversation opens automatically. No pressure, no algorithms pushing engagement — just genuine discovery.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

1. What We Collect

When you create an account, we collect: your name, username, email address, FIL Number, and optional profile photo and bio. Message content is stored in Firebase Realtime Database to enable real-time delivery.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and improve the messaging service
  • To display your profile to people you connect with
  • To power the Match discovery feature (only your name, photo, and bio are shown)

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure, subject to Google's security standards. Message images are stored via Cloudinary CDN.
